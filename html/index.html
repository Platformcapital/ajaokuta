What we do

Ajaokuta Gas Park (“AGP”), is building the first and largest gas park in Northern Nigeria. It is set to become a hub that caters for the natural gas needs of Northern Nigeria and beyond. The project consists of an LNG mini plant, a CNG Plant and an LPG plant.

  • We provide LNG to power plants and industries across Northern Nigeria. It’s cleaner than diesel and ideal for large-scale energy use.

  • Our CNG solutions power factories, transport fleets, and businesses within a 250km radius of the gas park — with scalable distribution.

  • We supply affordable domestic cooking gas to retailers and homes, helping reduce reliance on charcoal and kerosene.

Frequently asked questions

Discover how we tailor our energy solutions to meet diverse needs—delivering reliable gas supply, scalable infrastructure, and exceptional service to homes, businesses, and industries.

We produce and distribute Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG), Compressed Natural Gas (CNG), and Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) to homes, industries, and power facilities across Northern Nigeria.

We work with industrial clients, energy providers, retailers, distributors, and government partners. We currently do not supply directly to individuals but work with retailers who serve communities.

We are in advanced stages of project development with over 95% regulatory approvals secured. Phase 1 operations (LNG, CNG, LPG) will begin in Q1 2025.

The park is located in Idaku community, Ajaokuta LGA, Kogi State — just 1km off the Okene-Ajaokuta highway and near key pipeline and rail infrastructure.

Our park is the first of its kind in Northern Nigeria, combining LNG, CNG, and LPG production in one location. We are strategically positioned 40 meters from the NGC pipeline, allowing faster, more cost-effective distribution.
