About us

Ajaokuta Gas Park

Ajaokuta Gas Park is Northern Nigeria’s pioneering energy hub, built to meet the region’s growing demand for safe, reliable, and affordable natural gas. Situated on a 28-hectare site in Kogi State, we are reshaping access to clean energy for industries, transport systems, and households alike.

Through our integrated LNG, CNG, and LPG facilities, we connect critical infrastructure with underserved markets. We are more than a gas provider—we are a driver of industrial transformation, energy efficiency, and regional development. Every pipeline we lay and every delivery we make brings Nigeria one step closer to a sustainable energy future.
